## Why this matters more than the other XAUUSD candidates

Cross-asset generalization is the strongest robustness signal a strategy can show. Gen_Breakout has now produced positive edge on **two structurally different assets**:

| Asset | Regime | Gen_Breakout result |
|---|---|---|
| USDJPY | BoJ-driven yen weakness, slow uptrend punctuated by snapbacks | +$184K, PF 1.20, Sharpe 1.37 (QuantDash), DD 28.97% |
| XAUUSD | Multi-year secular bull, sharp consolidations | +$70K, est PF ~1.15, balanced long/short |

Crucially, Gen_Breakout is **not directional** — it trades both ways via the London-open OCO breakout. ChannelBreakoutVIP only worked on both assets because both trended up; Gen_Breakout extracts edge from a regime-agnostic structural pattern (London open volatility expansion).

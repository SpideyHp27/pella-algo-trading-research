## Why this is the most interesting result of the day

Every other XAUUSD candidate is the **trend-follower archetype**:
- 38-46% win rate
- 1.8-3.5× asymmetry
- Edge from outlier wins riding multi-bar trends
- Highly correlated with each other (same regime, same direction)

**TuesdayTurnaroundNDX is the inverse:**
- 58% win rate (catches the predicted move most of the time)
- 1.06× asymmetry (wins and losses are roughly the same size)
- Edge from pattern repeat / day-of-week seasonality
- Mechanism is fundamentally different from trend-following

This is exactly the **uncorrelated edge profile** the portfolio framework wants. If the correlation matrix confirms <0.35 with the trend-following crowd, this becomes a genuine diversifier — the kind that lowers portfolio drawdown without lowering returns.

## Risks / caveats

1. **141 trades over 6 years = ~24/year.** Pattern-based by design. Sample size is OK but smaller than the trend-followers. Higher noise on Sharpe/p-value computation.
2. **Pattern decay risk.** Day-of-week effects can fade as more participants exploit them. Robust IS/OOS check critical here — split 2020-2023 IS vs 2024-2026 OOS, see if the Tuesday effect is still firing post-2023.
3. **Long-only.** Captures upward-bias Tuesday dynamics. May not work in regime where Tuesdays mean-revert downward (extended bear market).
